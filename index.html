RUM

Rum is a distilled alcoholic beverage made
from sugarcane byproducts, such as molasses
or directly from sugarcane juice, by a process
of fermentation and distillaction. The distillate,
a clear liquid, is then usually aged in oak
barrels. Rum plays a part in the culture of

Most islands of the west indies, as well
as in The Maritimes and Newfoundland.
This Beverage has famous associaton with
the Royal Navy and piracy
